## [cite_start]SEWE [cite: 2]

### [cite_start]Numerical Modeling of Medical Imaging Modality Acquisition and Reconstruction Process (SBE 4220) [cite: 3]

[cite_start]The project targets the simulation of a medical imaging modality acquisition and reconstruction process demonstrating students' understanding of the process[cite: 4]. [cite_start]Simulations may include physics-based artifact modeling; motion; sampling, geometric and/or dose constraints that explore the trade-offs between radiation dose, noise levels, reconstruction methods and sparsity issues... etc[cite: 5]. [cite_start]The project may also demonstrate system advancements and reconstruction correction techniques[cite: 5]. [cite_start]Students are welcome to include other constructive ideas in their detailed project statement[cite: 6]. [cite_start]Targeted imaging modalities are: X-ray, CT, PET, or SPECT[cite: 7]. [cite_start]Upon proper referencing, students can build their projects upon existing open source simulators[cite: 7].
