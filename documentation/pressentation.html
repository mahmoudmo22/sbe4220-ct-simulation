Noise & Dose Modeling

Poisson Statistics: $$I_{measured} \sim \text{Poisson}(I_{expected})$$
The Variance Rule: $$\frac{\sigma}{\mu} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{I_0}}$$

Quantitative Evaluation Metrics

Spatial Resolution (MTF): $$\text{MTF}(f) = \left| \mathcal{F}\left\{\frac{d}{dx}\text{ESF}(x)\right\} \right|$$
Detectability (CNR): $$\text{CNR} = \frac{|\mu_{insert} - \mu_{bg}|}{\sigma_{bg}}$$
